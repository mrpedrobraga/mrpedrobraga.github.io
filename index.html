    Inner Voices『心からの声』

    by yours truly, me!

    Inner Voices is my indie RPG about the differences amongst human minds.

    Claire and her grow together as friends in the cozy comfort of New Horizon City.
    But, when a terrifying entity threatens their livelihoods, they must work together to understand the world from other perspectives.


    It tackles themes of Mental Health Conditions and Neurodiversity, with a commitment to faithfulness, but ultimately a light and fun experience.

    Don't cry until the end!

    Dreamed Away

    by Nicolas Petton

    I also contribued to 'Dreamed Away,' another RPG with a tangential similarity to Inner Voices.

    I helped design the combat system, enemies in it -- and even some music.

    Dreamed Away is an emotional action-adventure RPG set in France in the 90s.

    Play as Théo, a boy lost in a dark, mysterious world.
    Explore a unique reality, battle darkness in duels and remember: choices matter.
