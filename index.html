    Inner Voices『心からの声』

    by yours truly, me!

    Inner Voices is a genuine RPG about the differences amongst human minds. Abstract theme, I know, and it leads to vastly interesting gameplay.

    Every person is different, has unique needs and lives unique lives.
    And as a mysterious entity threatens their city, the heroes must work together to understand that.


    It tackles themes of Mental Health Conditions and Neurodiversity, with a commitment to faithfulness, but ultimately a goofy and fun experience.

    Dreamed Away

    by Nicolas Petton

    I also contribued to 'Dreamed Away,' another RPG with a tangential similarity to Inner Voices.
    I helped design the combat system, enemies in it -- and even some music.

    Dreamed Away is an emotional action-adventure RPG set in France in the 90s.
    Play as Théo, a boy lost in a dark, mysterious world.
    Explore a unique reality, battle darkness in duels and remember: choices matter.
