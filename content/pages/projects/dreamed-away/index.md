Dreamed Away is a videogame by [Nicolas Petton](https://nicolas.petton.fr/), who's also a computer nerd. His website is almost as cool as mine, so you should definitely check him out.

Dreamed Away is a top-down RPG, where all the battles are "duels" between you and an enemy. The combat system (which I had a small part in designing) has your player at the bottom of the screen dodging from left to right... That adds some action to a mostly strategy-focused battle! Some people compare this game to "UNDERTALE," whatever that is.

I've also composed a few tracks available in the [game's original soundtrack](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4265490/Dreamed_Away_Soundtrack/)... That's right: for some reason, Nicolas was interested in my music, even though that's not what anybody knows me for! Well, I did my best, and I think it turned out alright.
