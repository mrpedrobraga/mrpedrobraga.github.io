DREAMTONE is a traditional RPG, whimsically harkening back to the long-gone days of the 1980s. It’s a love letter to the wacky, strange, and sinister stories of the past while being something completely new. It’s a game about a world with too many dues to count, where the children foot the bill for the sake of the future. It’s about the power of dreams, and how those with twisted and warped dreams will use any means necessary to enact their will. It’s about a group of unlikely friends trekking across America, fighting demons and any monsters that cross their path. It’s about an American poster-boy beating up rats with his baseball bat and hitting projectiles back at his foes.

If you're excited for INNER VOICES, you'll enjoy this game a lot.

![Dreamtone Screenshot](https://dreamtonegame.com/Resources/Assets/Screenshots/screen4.png)

Most of my contributions to this have been the lead developer asking me to solve a certain bug, and, in the process of explaining the bug to me, solving it himself. Though I've done some coding, as well as suggestions... In fact, whenever you land a critical hit, and you see a huge "BAM!", think of me!

This game is being developed by **Not Gonna Miss It!**, a group of wonderfully creative folks, some of which futilely attempt to get me to work on fixing bugs... nevertheless, some of my code is in this game, and I get an "assistant programming" credit!

Volume 1 is coming very very soon, possibly before the end of the decade, so keep your eyes peeled!
