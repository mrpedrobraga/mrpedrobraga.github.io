About

Inner Voices is a psychological fantasy RPG, serialized like a TV show, with the finest pixel art graphics.

Play as Claire, a young psychic girl, as she embarks on a journey to help others figure out the nebulous world of their minds...

...and by that I mean diving into a new dimension, meeting strange characters, uncovering troubling memories and eating lots of greasy food!

The game will be released in 5 episodes
tackling diverse themes of mental health and neurodivergence, with a commitment to faithfulness and respect,
but still being fun and silly.

A Game For Everyone.

As a game so diverse, with a main theme of looking at the world through other perspectives, it was important to allow all those people to play...

Hence, the game is built around accessibility.
Sound controls, onomatopoeia, configurable battle system, optional progression cues & even play the whole game completely blind.
There's no excuse not to check it out ;)


(The website is still in development regarding accessibility.)

Modding

The game is built in Godot Engine using Star Engine as a framework.

It notoriously supports resource packs (mods) to be loaded, allowing you to replace or add new code, images, music, items, characters, battles, scenes, anything. They can, then, be packaged as .pcks and distributed to be loaded onto a working copy of the game.

To begin, follow The Inner Voices modding guide.
